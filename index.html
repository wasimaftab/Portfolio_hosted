A Bit About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Biomedical Center Munich, LMU Munich, specializing in bioinformatics and data analysis using R. My Ph.D. focused on computational methods for proteomics data analysis. My current research involves analyzing high-throughput '-omics' datasets to advance chromatin studies. Additionally, I conduct research in prompt engineering and knowledge graphs, and develop full-stack applications using HTML, CSS, JS, and Python to enhance the retrieval of information from biomedical research articles.
