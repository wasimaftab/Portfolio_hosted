A Bit About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Biomedical Center Munich, LMU Munich, where I specialize in leveraging cutting-edge bioinformatics and data analysis to unlock new insights in the realm of chromatin studies. With a Ph.D. rooted in advanced computational methods for proteomics data analysis, I am passionate about transforming complex, high-throughput '-omics' datasets into actionable knowledge. My work doesn't stop there—I’m also pushing the boundaries of research through innovative prompt engineering and knowledge graph applications. Beyond research, I harness my skills in full-stack development (HTML, CSS, JS, and Python) to create powerful tools that enhance the retrieval of critical information from biomedical research articles. Let’s connect and explore how my expertise can drive your next breakthrough!
